## Brand Consistency Guidelines

When adding new content, maintain:

1. **Tone:** Conversational, not corporate
2. **Depth:** Explain why, not just how
3. **Honesty:** Share learnings and mistakes openly
4. **Practicality:** Every post should be actionable
5. **Clarity:** Assume reader is smart but unfamiliar
6. **Authenticity:** Document your actual journey, not perfection
